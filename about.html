Our Story

Born in Seoul.
Built on Science.

BIOCESS was founded by a team of Korean cosmetic scientists and beauty innovators who shared a frustration: the gap between what skincare science could achieve and what most products actually delivered.

For years, the team researched hyaluronic acid at the molecular level — not just as an ingredient, but as a biological system. They discovered that targeting multiple skin layers simultaneously, with precisely calibrated molecular weights, produced dramatically superior results compared to conventional single-weight formulas.

The result was Spring Subun Ampoule — named after the Korean word for moisture (수분, 水分) and inspired by the renewal of spring. A product that doesn't just promise hydration but delivers it at every layer of your skin, every single day.

What We Believe

Brand Philosophy

Three core values guide every decision we make — from formula development to packaging design to how we communicate with our customers.

Science First
Every ingredient in our formula has a clearly defined, scientifically validated function. We don't add anything for aesthetics alone. INCI names and mechanism of action are available for every component, and we welcome scrutiny.
Radical Transparency
We publish our full ingredient list, explain what each ingredient does, and are upfront about what our product cannot do. We believe informed customers make the best skincare choices — and better choices lead to better skin outcomes.
Skin for Every Person
Hydration is universal. Whether your skin is oily, dry, sensitive, or combination — every skin type benefits from multi-layer moisture. We formulate inclusively, with a gentle profile that welcomes all skin types, tones, and ages.

Our Commitment

Sustainability &
Responsibility

We believe that the best skincare should not come at the cost of the planet. BIOCESS is committed to minimizing our environmental footprint at every step — from sourcing ingredients to the packaging that reaches your hands.

Our green glass bottle is not just beautiful — it's infinitely recyclable. We use minimum packaging materials, sourced sustainably, and are actively working toward a fully compostable outer box for future production runs.

Ask About Our Practices
Recyclable Green Glass
Our signature emerald glass bottles are 100% recyclable and designed for long-term use — refill programs planned for 2026.
Clean Formula
No parabens, no artificial colorants, no microplastics. Every ingredient passes our internal safety and environmental review.
Responsibly Sourced
All plant-derived ingredients — including centella asiatica — are sourced from certified sustainable farms.
Minimal Packaging
We use only what is necessary — FSC-certified paper for outer boxes, with zero single-use plastic inserts.
Cruelty-Free
BIOCESS products are never tested on animals. We adhere to cruelty-free standards throughout our entire supply chain.
Carbon Reduction Plan
We are mapping our full supply chain carbon footprint with a goal to achieve net-zero operations by 2030.
