Application Guide

How to Use

The 2-minute ritual that transforms your skin's hydration — morning and evening.

1
🧴
Cleanse
Start with your regular cleanser. Pat face nearly dry — leave skin slightly damp for best HA absorption
2
💧
Dispense
Drop 3–4 drops of ampoule onto your fingertips or directly onto face
3
🖐️
Press & Pat
Gently press and pat the serum across your entire face and neck — do not rub
4
⏳
Absorb
Wait 60 seconds for the formula to fully absorb into all 5 skin layers
5
🌿
Seal with Moisturizer
Follow with your moisturizer to lock in all the hydration from the ampoule
Pro Tip

Apply on slightly damp skin — hyaluronic acid binds to water molecules. Having surface moisture present significantly boosts absorption and effectiveness.

Usage Tips

Best Practices for Maximum Results

  • Use AM & PM for optimal 24-hour hydration coverage
  • Apply before SPF in the morning routine
  • Pairs exceptionally well with vitamin C serums and retinol
  • Layer under richer creams during harsh winter months
  • Safe for all skin types including sensitive and acne-prone
